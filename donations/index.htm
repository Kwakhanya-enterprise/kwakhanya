    Donations

    As the pandemic continues to impact us, both directly and indirectly, Kwakhanya Social Enterprise Foundation is inviting your family to take part in the spirit of ubuntu by helping to support another family through these challenging times and beyond.

    " Do a little bit of good where you are, its those little bits put together that overwhelms the world"

    by Bishop Tutu

    Kwakhanya Social Enterprise Foundation provides social work interventions that address the urgent needs of families, Orphans, Caregivers for the Elderly people, Volunteers, and assisting Students to secure Internships and Learners programs we also provide family therapy.

    ​We are asking for your support, in the spirit of ubuntu, to become a monthly donor to help support another family who is in severe need through these challenging times and beyond.

     

    Here’s how it works:

    Become a monthly donor (make sure to include your email address)

    Those who contribute R350 or more per month will receive:

    An overview of the family you are helping and their situation as soon as you have registered

    An update on your family’s progress and situation every second month by our social workers

    Got questions? Contact us at [email protected] 

    Sign up today!

    ​Your generous support will help a Child, Family, and Community to get back on their feet and will assist in the efforts to ensure that some of the most vulnerable families in South Africa are in a home that is stable and supportive for their children.

    Each of us is a piece of the puzzle.
